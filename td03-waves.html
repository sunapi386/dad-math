The Doom Curve

You may have noticed something unfair about Core Keep: the bugs get tougher faster than you get richer. That's not a bug — it's THE design. Tonight: how “+25% per wave” quietly becomes a rocket, why your coin income is merely a line, and the one-division trick that predicts the wave where every defense on Earth must fall.

⏱ ~30 min   percent growth · exponentials   📈 predict the doom wave

1 · The game's actual rule

Here is the line from the engine, word for word: hp = 14 × 1.25wave−1. Multiplying by 1.25 is the same as adding 25% — a quarter more, every wave, of the new amount. That “of the new amount” is everything. Watch it feed on itself:

wave12345678910
grunt HP141822273443536783104

From 14 to 104 — more than 7× — in ten waves, and no single step ever looked scary. Check the table: the HP roughly doubles every 3 waves (14 → 27 → 53 → 104). That's because 1.25^3 \approx 1.95 \approx 2. Growth that multiplies is called exponential, and its superpower is exactly this disguise: each step looks tame, the total is a monster.

2 · The race you cannot win

Meanwhile your side grows by addition: each wave's bounty buys you roughly one more Gunner — a fixed +6 DPS. Adding the same amount each step draws a straight line. And here is the theorem that runs every endless game: an exponential curve eventually crosses ANY line. Any slope. Always. The only question is when.

Notice the two ways out: set growth to 0% (the bugs give up on percents — now it's line vs line, and the faster line wins forever), or… there is no “or”. As long as the percent is positive, doom has a date. The designers of Core Keep chose +25% precisely so that date lands just past wave 10 — you're meant to win the war seconds before it becomes unwinnable. Now you know why endless modes are called “endless”: it's a joke at your expense.

3 · The banker's shortcut

Want the doubling time without a calculator? Bankers have used one division for centuries:

$$\text{waves to double} \approx \frac{72}{\text{percent growth}}$$

72 ÷ 25 ≈ 3 — our every-3-waves doubling, instantly. This rule of 72 is the same math as money earning interest (8% → doubles in ~9 years), a virus spreading (+25% a day → cases double every 3 days — remember?), and populations growing. One game rule, half the news explained.
