🏰 Core Keep

The bugs are marching on your crystal core. Build turrets beside the road, survive ten waves, and the keep is yours. Then comes the real level: every single thing this game does — knowing what's in range, pricing the turrets, growing the waves, choosing who to shoot — is math you can open up. Play first. 🏰 Defend the core

Pick a turret from the shop, then click a grass cell to build (you can't build on the road). Click a placed turret to change who it shoots — or sell it. When you're ready: Start wave.

💰 50 ❤️ 10 🌊 wave 1/10 ☠️ 0 🏆 best –
Build a turret or two, then send in wave 1.
Field manual: corners are gold — a turret tucked inside a bend of the road keeps bugs in range around two sides. The 🔫 Gunner is your bread and butter. ❄️ Cryo barely scratches, but everything it touches walks at half speed through your other guns. 💣 Mortar shells splash — they hit the clump, not the bug. And if a turret is in a bad spot, sell it: you get 75% back (why 75%? sell one and check the refund — that's a percent problem you're living in).

📡 How does a turret KNOW a bug is in range?

There's a circle around every turret that's never drawn wrong. One line of math checks it — and that line never takes a square root. Pythagoras runs this game.
