📓 The Homework Check

Did the lesson stick? Every page on the site has a check here — 5 fresh random questions, get 4 or more right and that lesson is marked ✅ on your progress (worth ⭐⭐ the first time). Pass all five math checks for the 🎓 Homework Hero badge — and every check on this page for 🧑‍🎓 Professor. Retake any check, any day: the questions are always new, so there's nothing to memorize.

📋 Your assignments
For grown-ups: assign the week's homework and check it off here before game time. Assigning an item resets it to “to do” — your kid has to pass it again, stamped with the time, so you're verifying today's work.

Four checks live elsewhere on purpose: 🐛 Aim & Hitboxes is a skill challenge on its own page (you have to actually hit the weak points), the 🌳 Builder's Tree nodes are unlocked by their own principles tests, the 🖋️ Sketch Studio checks itself off when you've rendered all five scenes with the engine, and 🏰 Core Keep checks itself off when you hold all ten waves in the game.
