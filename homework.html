📓 The Homework Check

Did the lesson stick? Each check asks 5 fresh random questions — get 4 or more right and that lesson is marked ✅ on this device (worth ⭐⭐ the first time). Pass all five for the 🎓 Homework Hero badge. You can retake any check, any day — the questions are always new, so there's nothing to memorize.

For grown-ups: this is the “validation” step — the paper homework builds the skill, this confirms it. If a check fails twice, go back and replay the lesson's widgets together rather than retrying immediately.

Checks cover the five math lessons. CS, graph theory and story-mode quizzes live inside their own pages.
